MTM

Medication management therapy is defined as “a service or group of services that optimize therapeutic outcomes for individual patients.” These services include options such as:

  • Medication therapy reviews
  • Pharmacotherapy consults
  • Disease management coach and support
  • Pharmacogenomics Applications
  • Over-the-counter Medications
  • Immunizations
  • Health and wellness programs
  • Medication safety Surveillance
  • New Prescriptions
  • Transfer Prescriptions
